A Goblin horde from the west is bearing down on the city!

The king is enlisting people to help defend the evacuation. Do you have what it takes to keep the raid at bay while the citizens escape?

It's up to you to stop the goblins from crossing the bridge into the city!

Gameplay:

  • Up/Down/Left/Right keys to move in the respective direction.
  • Space bar to stab with your spear. (hold for rapid stabbiness)
  • Getting hit by a Goblin will cost you 1
  • Letting a Goblin past the bridge will also cost you 1
